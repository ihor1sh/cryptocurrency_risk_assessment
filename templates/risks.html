Risk Categories

Technical Risks

Technical risks include software vulnerabilities, the possibility of cyberattacks, problems with blockchain network scalability, as well as risks of losing private keys.

  • Smart contract vulnerabilities
  • 51% attacks on blockchain networks
  • Performance and transaction speed issues
  • Loss of access to funds due to key loss

Economic Risks

Cryptocurrency price volatility creates significant economic risks for public budgets. In addition, there are risks of losing funds due to errors or fraud.

  • Cryptocurrency price volatility
  • Liquidity risks
  • Possibility of losing public funds
  • Impact on fiscal policy

Legal and Regulatory Risks

Legal uncertainty, lack of clear regulatory mechanisms and international standards create additional challenges for public institutions.

  • Lack of clear legal regulation
  • International jurisdiction conflicts
  • Complexity of tracking and taxation
  • Issues with liability and insurance

Operational Risks

Operational risks are related to managing cryptocurrency assets, training personnel and integrating new technologies into existing public systems.

  • Complexity of managing cryptocurrency assets
  • Need for staff training
  • Integration problems with existing systems
  • Dependence on technical providers
