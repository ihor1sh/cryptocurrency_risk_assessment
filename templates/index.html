About the Research

Cryptocurrencies and blockchain technologies are gaining increasing popularity in various areas of life, including the public sector. However, their use in government structures is associated with numerous risks that require careful analysis and assessment.

Image: Integration of cryptocurrency technologies into public systems

Integration of cryptocurrency technologies into public systems

Main Research Directions:

  • Technical security risks
  • Economic and financial risks
  • Legal and regulatory challenges
  • Operational risks
  • Reputational risks

Image: Analysis of blockchain technology risks

Analysis of blockchain technology risks

Research Objective

The objective of this research is a comprehensive analysis of risks associated with the use of cryptocurrencies and blockchain technologies in the public sector, development of a methodology for their assessment, and formulation of recommendations for minimizing negative consequences.
