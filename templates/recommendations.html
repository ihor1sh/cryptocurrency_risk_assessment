Recommendations

Strategic Recommendations

Development of a Comprehensive Security Strategy

It is necessary to create a multi-layered protection system that includes technical means, key management procedures, regular security audits, and staff training.

Formation of a Legal Framework

Development and adoption of clear legislation regulating the use of cryptocurrencies in the public sector, including taxation, reporting, and liability issues.

Creation of Specialized Units

Formation of separate structural units for managing cryptocurrency assets with qualified personnel and clear procedures.

Implementation of a Monitoring System

Development and implementation of a continuous risk monitoring system that will allow prompt detection and response to potential threats.

International Cooperation

Active participation in international cryptocurrency regulation initiatives and exchange of experience with other countries to develop best practices.

Practical Steps

  1. Conducting pilot projects with limited scope
  2. Creating an interdepartmental working group
  3. Development of standards and protocols
  4. Training and professional development of personnel
  5. Gradual scaling after evaluation of results
